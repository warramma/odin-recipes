My Favorite Malaysian Recipes

For me, one of the best things about being Malaysian is the incredible food. As a sweet-tooth, I especially love the desserts. Here are three of my favorite childhood snacks that never fail to brighten my day with their amazing taste and flavor.

  • Kuih Bahulu
  • Tart Nenas (Pineapple Tart)
  • Bubur Pulut Hitam (Black Sticky Rice Pudding)
